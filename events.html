Shooting a watch with "CHRON"

For this event, CHRON will help an activity of using one light and a lot of DIY reflectors and flags to shoot a watch. The second way involved using a lightpainting technique to photograph a watch. To make it more interesting we will use iPads and smartphone as light sources.We will have more than 50 types of watches for you and you can bring your own cameras, we will provide you water and snacks.

  • DATE: March 18, 2017
  • TIME: 10:00 - 15:00 PDT
  • LOCATION: 2377 Scott road Surrey BC V6T O9B
  • PRICE: FREE for CHRON member, $12 for non-member
