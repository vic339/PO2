Our Story

Chron

A chronozone or chron is a slice of time that begins at a given identifiable event and ends at another. Our watches are made from the finest materials and are carefully crafted with attention to every detail.

Design philosophy

Chron is a series of watch with an unique dial which rotates around its own axis. It is build with high quality and is permanently in motion. With their graceful case, ultra thin mesh leather straps, your Chron watch will carry you through the day and into the night with confidence and ease.
